What happens next

Your request stays clear from the beginning

MoldWise helps organize your request for comparison. The final service conversation happens between you and any participating provider you choose to continue with.

  • Submit your request details

    Share the concern type, contact details, location context, and message.

  • MoldWise receives the request

    Your submitted information helps route the request context.

  • Participating providers may contact you

    Providers may discuss availability, scope, pricing details, and next steps.

  • You compare available options

    Review provider terms and verify licensing or insurance where required.

  • Final agreement is between you and provider
