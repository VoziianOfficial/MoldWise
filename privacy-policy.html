This Privacy Policy explains how MoldWise may collect, use, and share information submitted through this independent provider-matching website.

MoldWise is not a mold remediation contractor, inspection company, testing laboratory, medical provider, or emergency service.

Overview

MoldWise operates as an independent provider-matching platform. The website helps users submit mold-related requests so they may compare available participating provider options. MoldWise does not directly perform mold remediation, mold inspection, mold testing, repairs, cleanup, prevention work, or medical services.

By using this website or submitting a request form, you acknowledge that information you provide may be used to process your request and may be shared with participating providers for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

Information We Collect

We may collect information that you voluntarily submit through the website, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Selected service category or mold-related concern type.
  • Message content describing the concern, property context, or request details.
  • Consent checkbox confirmation and form submission metadata.
  • Technical information such as IP address, browser type, device data, and pages viewed.

Please do not submit sensitive medical information, emergency information, passwords, payment card numbers, or other information that is not necessary for a provider-matching request.

How We Use Information

Information submitted through this website may be used to:

  • Receive and process your mold-related provider-matching request.
  • Route your request details to participating providers where applicable.
  • Respond to questions or support requests.
  • Improve website performance, usability, security, and compliance.
  • Maintain records related to submissions, consent, and website operations.
  • Prevent spam, abuse, fraud, or unauthorized website use.

How Information May Be Shared

MoldWise may share submitted request information with participating independent providers so they may contact you about availability, scope, pricing details, scheduling, and service terms. These providers are independent from MoldWise and are responsible for their own services, communications, representations, pricing, and agreements.

Information may also be shared with website hosting providers, email delivery services, analytics tools, security tools, legal advisors, or other service providers that help operate this website. We may disclose information if required by law or when needed to protect rights, safety, security, or legal interests.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

This website may use cookies or similar technologies to remember cookie consent choices, support basic website functionality, improve performance, and understand general usage. You can adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies, though some website features may not work as intended.

For more information, please review the Cookie Policy.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit a request form. You may also contact MoldWise to ask about information you previously submitted, request an update, or request deletion where applicable and legally permitted.

If a participating provider contacts you after a request is submitted, any further communications, agreements, or opt-out requests with that provider should be handled directly with that provider.

Security

MoldWise uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, email transmission, or online storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

This website is intended for use by adults. MoldWise does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through this website, please contact us so the information can be reviewed.
