Service questions

Black mold concern FAQs

Does MoldWise identify black mold?

No. MoldWise does not identify, test, diagnose, or inspect. Participating providers handle their own evaluations and terms.

What should I include in the request?

Include location, visible appearance, moisture history, odor, and whether the affected area is changing.

Can I ask providers about testing?

Yes. Ask participating providers whether testing is recommended and what their approach includes.

Is MoldWise a medical provider?

No. MoldWise does not provide medical guidance or health-related guarantees.

Who determines the final scope?

Participating providers determine their own proposed scope and terms.

Should I compare multiple options?

Yes. Comparing options can help you review availability, scope, pricing, and provider terms.
