Service questions

Basement mold request FAQs

Does MoldWise fix basement mold directly?

No. MoldWise is an independent provider-matching platform and does not perform basement services.

What basement details help?

Mention musty odors, damp walls, visible spots, water history, humidity, and affected materials.

Can providers discuss moisture control?

Provider approaches vary, so ask each provider about moisture control or prevention planning.

Can availability vary?

Yes. Availability, scope, pricing, and service terms vary by provider and location.

Does submitting a request create an agreement?

No. Final agreements are between the homeowner and participating provider.

Should I verify provider details?

Yes. Verify licensing, insurance, and provider terms where required.
