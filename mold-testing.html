Service questions

Mold testing request FAQs

Does MoldWise perform mold testing?

No. MoldWise does not perform testing directly. Participating providers handle testing-related services and terms.

Can I ask about air or surface testing?

Yes. Include your testing questions in the request so providers can discuss their approach.

Do all providers test the same way?

No. Testing approach, reporting, pricing, and timing may vary by provider.

Who explains the testing terms?

Participating providers explain their own testing terms, scope, and pricing.

Does testing create a remediation agreement?

No. Any service agreement is between you and the provider you choose.

Should I compare reporting details?

Yes. Ask about what type of report or follow-up information may be included.
