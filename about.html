Platform mission

A more organized starting point for mold-related requests

MoldWise is an independent provider-matching platform created to help homeowners describe mold-related concerns clearly, compare available local provider options, and stay informed before moving forward.

We are not a mold remediation company, inspection company, testing company, contractor, repair provider, medical provider, manufacturer, or retailer. Participating providers handle their own availability, scope, pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms.

Homeowners should review provider details carefully and verify licensing and insurance where required before making a final decision.
