Platform mission

A more organized starting point for mold-related requests

MoldWise is an independent provider-matching platform created to help homeowners describe mold-related concerns clearly, compare available local provider options, and stay informed before moving forward.

We are not a mold remediation company, inspection company, testing company, contractor, repair provider, medical provider, manufacturer, or retailer. Participating providers handle their own availability, scope, pricing, scheduling, warranties, licensing, insurance, and service terms.

Homeowners should review provider details carefully and verify licensing and insurance where required before making a final decision.

Why compare providers

The same concern can lead to different provider approaches

Comparing options helps you ask better questions before continuing. Look beyond one detail and review the full provider conversation.

  • Scope of work
  • Testing approach
  • Moisture source discussion
  • Availability
  • Pricing terms
  • Warranty and provider terms

Scope of work

Ask what the provider says is included, what is not included, and what may change after an on-site discussion.

Testing approach

If testing is part of the conversation, compare how each provider explains testing type, reporting, timing, and cost.

Moisture source discussion

Mold-related concerns often connect to moisture. Ask how providers discuss leaks, humidity, ventilation, or recurring dampness.

Availability

Availability can vary by location, provider, season, and requested service category.

Pricing terms

Ask how pricing is structured, what details are estimated, and what is confirmed directly by the provider.

Warranty and provider terms

Review provider terms carefully and verify licensing, insurance, and warranty details where required.
