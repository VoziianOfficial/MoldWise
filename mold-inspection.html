Service questions

Mold inspection request FAQs

Does MoldWise inspect homes?

No. MoldWise does not inspect homes directly. It helps connect users with participating provider options.

What can I describe in the request?

Describe visible signs, musty smells, water stains, room location, and any recent leaks.

Can inspection and testing be separate?

Provider approaches vary, so you should ask each provider what is included.

Will providers contact me?

Participating providers may contact you to discuss availability and next steps.

Does submitting a request commit me?

No. Submitting a request does not create a service agreement.

Who handles final terms?

Final pricing, scheduling, and terms are handled by participating providers.
