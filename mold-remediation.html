Service questions

Mold remediation request FAQs

Does MoldWise remove mold?

No. MoldWise does not perform remediation. It helps homeowners compare participating provider options.

What details should I include?

Include room type, visible growth, odor, moisture history, affected materials, and approximate area size.

Who provides the final price?

Final pricing is provided by the participating provider, not MoldWise.

Can I ask about moisture sources?

Yes. Ask providers how they discuss moisture sources and prevention-related follow-up.

Do providers vary by area?

Yes. Availability and terms may vary by location and provider.

Should I verify credentials?

Yes. Homeowners should verify licensing, insurance, and provider details where required.
