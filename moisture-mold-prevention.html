Service questions

Moisture and prevention request FAQs

Does MoldWise prevent mold directly?

No. MoldWise does not perform moisture control, prevention, inspection, or remediation services directly.

What moisture details should I share?

Share humidity, condensation, dampness, odors, ventilation issues, and the rooms affected.

Can I ask about ventilation?

Yes. Include ventilation or airflow concerns so providers can explain whether they discuss those topics.

Can prevention require inspection first?

Provider approaches vary, so ask each provider what they recommend before prevention planning.

Does submitting a request create a contract?

No. Final agreements are between you and the provider you choose.

Should provider details be verified?

Yes. Homeowners should verify licensing, insurance, and provider terms where required.
